Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2025

SAP Architect Prep ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (https://www.saparchitectprep.com) or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, email address, and payment details when you register or subscribe to our services.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our platform, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and session duration.
  • Learning Data: Practice test results, progress tracking, and preferences to personalize your learning experience.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Data collected through cookies for analytics and functionality purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To personalize your learning experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, or changes to our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party service providers to enhance our platform's functionality. These providers may process your personal data on our behalf under strict privacy agreements.

a. Usercentrics
We use Usercentrics for automated privacy compliance management. This tool ensures that we obtain and manage user consent in accordance with global privacy laws such as GDPR.

b. OpenRouter
We integrate OpenRouter to enable AI-powered chat functionality using open-source large language models (LLMs). OpenRouter processes user queries securely and temporarily for generating AI responses. No personally identifiable information (PII) is shared with OpenRouter unless explicitly provided by the user during their interaction.

c. Payment Processing
We use Stripe for secure payment processing. Stripe's Privacy Policy governs the handling of your payment details.

d. Analytics
We use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand user behavior and improve our platform. These tools collect anonymized data about how users interact with our website.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest to protect against unauthorized access. We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze usage patterns:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the operation of the site.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform.

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings or through Usercentrics' consent management tool.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data where applicable.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data collection at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@saparchitectprep.com.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers mentioned above for operational purposes.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our Terms of Service.

8. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of Australia where our third-party service providers operate. These transfers are conducted in compliance with applicable laws to ensure adequate protection of your data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or our practices. Significant updates will be communicated via email or notifications on our platform.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Email: support@saparchitectprep.com
